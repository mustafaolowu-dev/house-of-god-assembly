About us

A welcoming online Christian ministry in Hanover Park, Illinois.

House of God Assembly is a Christ-centered ministry serving Hanover Park, nearby Illinois communities, and online viewers through worship, prayer, biblical preaching, Bible study, and fellowship.

The ministry currently operates primarily online while trusting God for a future worship building. Whether you are looking for livestream worship, Holy Spirit Bible teaching, or a place to request prayer, you are welcome here.
