# Welcome to House of God Assembly Online

House of God Assembly is a Christ-centered ministry in Hanover Park, Illinois sharing worship, prayer, Bible teaching, and Christian sermons online.

## A ministry built on prayer and the Word

Our desire is to help people grow in faith, understand scripture, and stay connected through livestream worship and Holy Spirit Bible teaching.

## Watch and grow with us

- Join the Sunday worship service livestream.
- Watch Christian sermons online.
- Submit a prayer request.
- Share Bible teaching articles with family and friends.

We are trusting God for a future worship building while continuing to serve people online with love, clarity, and faith.
